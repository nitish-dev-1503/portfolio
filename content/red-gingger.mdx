Red Gingger is an e-commerce brand based in India and is committed to deliver quality fashion products. The idea behind the brand name was to have something which is as fresh as nature and as unique as a Red Gingger flower, which a very few of us know in India. 

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/portfolio-nitishsharma.appspot.com/o/designing%2FRedGingger%2012%20Artboard%2012.jpg?alt=media&token=1b8e312a-1bbb-4e14-ba36-59c7051d417a&_gl=1*jo4nxm*_ga*NDIwOTQwNDM0LjE2OTYxMzk3MTE.*_ga_CW55HF8NVT*MTY5NzM3NTA0OC40LjEuMTY5NzM3NjMwNC41MC4wLjA.)

#### The challenge
The challenge was to come with a brand identity which is modern and unique. The aim was to create something which goes with the style of current generation youth and showcase freshness inspired by nature. 

#### The Result
With a lot of brainstorming discussions with the RedGingger team, I was able to come up with a unique logo design and a unique typography for 'RedGingger'. It is minimal, modern and has an element of nature which shows freshness and originality.

Choosing the right set of colors was again a challenge, but we aggreed upon using the same natural color which a red gingger flower has.

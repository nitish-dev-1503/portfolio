##### Background
Red Gingger, an Indian e-commerce brand, is dedicated to delivering quality fashion products. The brand draws inspiration from the freshness of nature and the uniqueness of the Red Gingger flower, a relatively lesser-known bloom in India.

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/portfolio-nitishsharma.appspot.com/o/designing%2FRedGingger%2012%20Artboard%2012.jpg?alt=media&token=1b8e312a-1bbb-4e14-ba36-59c7051d417a&_gl=1*jo4nxm*_ga*NDIwOTQwNDM0LjE2OTYxMzk3MTE.*_ga_CW55HF8NVT*MTY5NzM3NTA0OC40LjEuMTY5NzM3NjMwNC41MC4wLjA.)

##### Challenge
The challenge was to create a modern and unique brand identity that resonates with the style of the current generation youth. The goal was to capture the essence of freshness inspired by nature and reflect the uniqueness associated with the Red Gingger flower.

##### Result
Through extensive discussions and brainstorming sessions with the Red Gingger team, a distinctive brand identity was crafted. The logo design and typography for 'Red Gingger' embody minimalism, modernity, and a touch of nature, projecting freshness and originality.

##### Key Features
<u>Unique Logo Design:</u> A logo that combines simplicity with a touch of nature, symbolizing freshness and originality.

<u>Typography:</u> Modern and distinctive typography for the brand name 'Red Gingger,' aligning with the preferences of the current generation.

<u>Color Palette:</u> Natural colors inspired by the Red Gingger flower, creating a cohesive and harmonious brand identity.
